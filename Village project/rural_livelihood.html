Rural Livelihood Project

Empowering rural communities through sustainable livelihood development, skills training, and microenterprise support.

Learn More

Our Approach

We enable marginalized communities to build skills, manage resources, and diversify their livelihoods for sustainable development.

Community Mobilization

We facilitate community mobilization, helping women's self-help groups and farmer producer organizations develop leadership skills and access resources.

Skills Development

Providing training in various skills and trades that enhance employment opportunities and encourage entrepreneurship in rural areas.

Natural Resource Management

Working with communities to develop sustainable agricultural practices, water management, and forest conservation strategies.

Key Achievements

Sustainable Agriculture

Trained 10,000+ farmers in sustainable agricultural practices, improving crop yields and reducing dependence on chemical fertilizers.

Women's Collectives

Established 1,200+ women's self-help groups that now manage microenterprises and community resources.

Market Linkages

Created 40+ market linkages for farmers to sell their produce directly, increasing household incomes by 30-40%.
