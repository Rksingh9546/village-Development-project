Key Goals

Our comprehensive environmental system aims to transform village ecosystems through sustainable practices and community involvement

Sustainable Practices

Promote eco-friendly practices that ensure long-term environmental sustainability

Clean Environment

Ensure clean air, water, and soil for better health and well-being

Green Cover

Increase plantation and maintain forest cover to enhance biodiversity

Waste Management

Implement effective waste segregation, collection, and recycling systems

Awareness

Educate and involve the community in environmental protection activities

Main Components of the Environment System

Tree Plantation Drives

  • Competitions for best garden

  • Regular waste audits

Recycling Centers

Rainwater Harvesting
