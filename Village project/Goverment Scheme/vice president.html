VICE-PRESIDENT OF INDIA

Name: Shri Jagdeep Dhankhar

Father's Name: Shri Gokal Chand

Date of Birth: May 18, 1951

Place of Birth: Kithana, Jhunjhunu district, Rajasthan

Marital Status: Married

Spouse's Name: Dr. Sudesh Dhankhar

Children: One daughter

Educational Qualifications: B.Sc., LL.B.

Profession: Lawyer, Politician

Career Highlights:

  • Governor of West Bengal (2019–2022)
  • Member of Parliament, Lok Sabha (1989–1991)
  • Minister of State for Parliamentary Affairs (1990–1991)
  • Elected as Vice President of India and ex-officio Chairman of Rajya Sabha on August 11, 2022

Awards: Notable contributions to legal and political fields
