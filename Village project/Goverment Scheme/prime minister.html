PRIME MINISTER OF INDIA

Shri Narendra Modi

Shri Narendra Modi is the current Prime Minister of India, having assumed office on May 26, 2014, and re-elected for a second term on May 30, 2019. Renowned for his dynamic leadership, dedication, and vision for a developed India, he has emerged as one of the most influential and popular leaders both nationally and globally.

Born on 17 September 1950 in Vadnagar, Gujarat, Shri Modi rose from humble beginnings, working tirelessly from a young age and dedicating his life to public service. As the Chief Minister of Gujarat (2001–2014), he transformed the state into a model of development and good governance, emphasizing infrastructure, industrial growth, and social welfare.

As Prime Minister, Shri Modi has launched numerous landmark initiatives, including Make in India, Digital India, Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Clean India Mission), and Ayushman Bharat. His leadership during challenging times, such as the COVID-19 pandemic, has been marked by swift decision-making and efforts to safeguard lives and livelihoods.

Internationally, Shri Modi has strengthened India’s global standing through active diplomacy, strategic partnerships, and initiatives like the International Solar Alliance. His strong presence on social media makes him one of the world’s most followed leaders online, reflecting his emphasis on direct communication with citizens.

In addition to his political achievements, Shri Modi is an author and poet. He practices yoga and meditation, which help him maintain a disciplined and balanced lifestyle despite a demanding schedule.
