Our Healthcare Services

Primary Health Centers

Local healthcare facilities offering general checkups, maternal care, and minor treatments. Our PHCs are equipped with basic diagnostic equipment and medicines.

Telemedicine

Connect with specialist doctors from city hospitals through video consultations. Ideal for follow-up appointments and specialist consultations.

Mobile Health Units

Our mobile unit brings healthcare to remote areas weekly, offering checkups, medicines, and basic treatments to those who cannot travel to PHCs.

Maternal & Child Care

Special focus on pregnant women and children with regular checkups, immunization programs, and nutrition counseling.

Health Insurance

Access to government schemes like Ayushman Bharat providing free or low-cost treatment at empaneled hospitals.

Health Awareness

Regular campaigns on hygiene, nutrition, vaccination, family planning, and disease prevention.
